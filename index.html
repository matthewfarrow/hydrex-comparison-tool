Why do users choose Hydrex over Aerodrome and Uniswap?

What is Omni-Liquidity?

Omni-Liquidity allows users to earn from multiple liquidity sources, including Uniswap pools, Hydrex pools, and other LP types, maximizing earning potential across the entire Base ecosystem.

Can I use Hydrex without a crypto wallet?

What makes Hydrex different from other DEXs?

Hydrex is specifically designed to bring new user segments onchain with features like social login, fiat onramp, Apple Pay, single-sided liquidity, and 100% revenue sharing. It's built as a public good on Base to support protocols that existing DEXs may not accommodate.

How secure is the Hydrex platform?

Hydrex is built on Base, which inherits Ethereum's security. The platform uses smart contracts that are audited and follows best practices for DeFi security. All transactions are on-chain and transparent.
